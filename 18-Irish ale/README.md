## Recipe
Grain bill:
* 11.25 lb British pale (bumped up to 11.8)
* 6 oz Crystal 40
* 6 oz Crystal 120
* 6 oz Roasted barley

Mash & Boil:
* Mash at 153 F for 60 minutes
* Boil 60 minutes

Boil additions:
* 1.25 oz. Kent Goldings (60 min)
* Irish moss (15 min)

Yeast:
* Wyeast 1084 Irish Ale (1)

Gravity targets:
* OG: 1.054
* Final: 1.014
